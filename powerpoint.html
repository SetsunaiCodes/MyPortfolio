Make it interactive!

Esspecially in High-school there is nothing that is more boring than static presentations. Text on a plane background, not even bulletpoints, just a copied text. This is bad. A good presentation contains bulletpoints and pictures every important aspect in a creative way, that isn't text. Charts in every shape, images, Bubbles and the topping: Clickable items! In the following I will show you some of my examples to explain further what I mean.

How it all started

During my last year of Abitur I had to give lots of presentations in English class. I wanted to spice things up and made my research on interacitve Power Point Presentations and learned a lot since then. But here I want to share my first attempt on creating an interactive Presentation. Here you can see a slider on the left side. You can click the symbols to directly jump to the next aspect of the presentation.
