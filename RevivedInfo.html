What is Revived?

I am super proud to announce this! Revived is a blog fully founded by myself! Every two days I publish new content about the latest games, trailers, series and movies for geeks like I am one. My intention behind Revived was quiet simple... I like talking about any topic that I am passionate for. There is no better to do so than creating a blog and add a comment section.

Important to mention is that Revived is wirtten in German. German is my mothertongue. Revived should be as authentic as possible. There is an english Version in progress tho!
